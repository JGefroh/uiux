Labels - Consider Label Alignment

There's several thoughts on the position of labels: to the left of the input field or directly above it. While there are benefits and drawbacks of each method, I personally prefer top-aligned labels.

Example

These labels are to the left of the input field and right-aligned. It takes more horizontal space, but it is generally easy to figure out what's expected.

A downside is that your eye must move back and forth between the label and the input field as you fill out the form.

Example (Preferred)

These labels are directly above the input field. It takes more vertical space, but it is easy to scan and figure out what is expected.

While you fill out the form, your eye moves in a single direction - downward.
