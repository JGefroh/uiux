Labels - Keep Related Things Close Together

Labels should be placed immediately next to the field they describe.

Example (Bad)

These left-aligned labels are far away from their respective input fields. It's hard to match up the labels with their respective input fields.

One of the labels is long - you'd have to break it up or abbreviate it to keep things aligned.

Example (Good)
By right-aligning the labels, we've closely associated them with their respective input boxes.
