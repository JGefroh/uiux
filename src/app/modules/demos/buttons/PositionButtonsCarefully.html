Position Buttons Carefully

Typical users have certain expectations in button placement. Avoid breaking those expectations.

Example (Bad)

 Close me

This modal's close button is on the top left instead of the traditional top right that many computer users are used to.

Another pitfall is that the "Next" button is to the left of the "Cancel" button. Traditionally, going to the "next" section and the right side of the screen are closely associated to give an illusion of progress.

Example (Good)

 Close me

This modal's close button is on the top right - as expected.

The next and cancel buttons are in their traditional order, too.

Example (Best)

 Close me

This modal's cancel button is WAY to the left, avoiding accidental clicks and further emphasizing that "Next" means progress.
